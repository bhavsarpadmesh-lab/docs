# WABAs

A **WhatsApp Business Account (WABA)** is your Meta business identity linked to InboundSage. Connect it in the [dashboard](https://app.inboundsage.com) to get `waba_id` and `phone_number_id` values for the API. List connected WABAs with `GET /v1/wabas`.
